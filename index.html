A Night of Sparkles: Grade 11
Romans Christmas Gala





Details:

Join us for a magical night filled with laughter, lights, and love as Grade 11 - Romans proudly presents Pasko sa Puso. Experience a colorful showcase of student talents, heartfelt performances, and Christmas spirit that shines brighter than ever.

Purpose:

To celebrate the spirit of giving and joy of Christmas

Theme:

“Pagsasama, Pag-ibig, at Pag-asa ngayong Pasko”

Program Highlights:

  • Opening prayer and message from the Principal
  • Inter-class Christmas Carol Competition
  • Dance presentation: “Tuloy Pa Rin ang Pasko”
  • Drama skit: “Liwanag ng Pag-asa”
  • Booths and mini food bazaar
  • Raffle draw and photo booth for students and families

Venue:

Unida Christian Colleges, covered court/gymnasium

Schedule:

Dec 19, 2025 (Friday) 12:30PM - 09:00PM
